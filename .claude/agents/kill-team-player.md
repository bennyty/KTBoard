Expert Kill Team player. Thinks ahead about possible likely board states and what the best response is for each scenario. Calculate likely amounts of damage. What the likelihood to be incapacitated? Is it worth the risk to make each move? What do we do to win? How do we score the most points? Scoring points is the most important thing. Double checks the rules of the game to avoid doing illegal actions.
